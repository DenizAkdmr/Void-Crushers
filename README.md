# **Void Vanguard**

System Alert: massive energy signature detected in Sector 7\.  
Mission: Survive the onslaught. Defeat the Titan.

**Void Vanguard** is a high-octane, single-file HTML5 space shooter. Built with vanilla JavaScript and the HTML5 Canvas API, it features a robust campaign, complex enemy AI, branching weapon upgrades, and intense boss battles.

## **üöÄ Features**

### **Core Gameplay**

* **7-Sector Campaign:** Battle through escalating difficulty levels, from the drone-infested outer rim to the Titan-controlled deep space.  
* **Branching Upgrade System:** Upon reaching Weapon Level 3, choose your tactical path:  
  * **Plasma Spread:** Wide-angle fire for crowd control.  
  * **Kinetic Assault:** Concentrated beams for melting single targets.  
* **Smart Pity System:** The ship's computer automatically reroutes power to weapon systems if you fail to find upgrades in a sector.  
* **High-Fidelity Visuals:** All assets are procedurally drawn using Canvas API (no external image assets required).

### **Hangar & Database**

* **Interactive Hangar:** Customize your ship's livery with 5 different neon color schemes before launching.  
* **Enemy Database:** A fully animated gallery allowing you to inspect enemy models and read tactical intel on every threat in the galaxy.

## **üëæ Enemy Intel**

### **Standard Hostiles**

* **Drone Mk.II:** Reinforced industrial cubes repurposed for swarm tactics.  
* **Interceptor:** Fast, swept-wing strike craft.  
* **Lancer:** High-velocity kinetic rammers. Do not let them get close.  
* **Tri-Striker:** Heavy dread-fighters armed with V-split cannons.  
* **Prism Frigate:** Support cruisers that charge and fire high-intensity laser beams.

### **Boss Encounters**

1. **Sector 3: Cruiser Vanguard**  
   * *Class:* Stealth Bomber  
   * *Threat:* High. Fires plasma orbs and deploys gravity bombs.  
2. **Sector 5: Phoenix Destroyer**  
   * *Class:* Bi-Hull Prototype  
   * *Threat:* Critical. Capable of splitting its hull into two independent attack modules when damaged.  
3. **Sector 7: Titan Dreadnought**  
   * *Class:* Mobile Fortress  
   * *Threat:* Extinction Level. Heavily armored, equipped with a Gatling array and the "Planet Cracker" main laser.
